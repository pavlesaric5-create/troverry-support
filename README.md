  ## Frequently Asked Questions

  ### How do I add an asset?
  Tap the "+" button on the home screen, fill in the asset details, and add photos.

  ### How do I delete an asset?
  Swipe left on any asset in the home screen and tap the delete button.

  ### Is my data safe?
  Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device with Face ID/PIN protection. We do not collect or transmit any data.

  ### Can I backup my data?
  Currently, all data is stored locally. Cloud sync will be available in a future update.

  ### How do I change the language?
  Go to Settings and select your preferred language.

  ## Privacy

  TroVerry does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All information stays on your device.
